Our Story

The man behind the pasta.

Stella Marina is not a restaurant in the conventional sense. There is no printed menu, no team of waiters, no shortcuts. There is only Valentino — who arrives each morning, makes his pasta by hand, and greets every guest himself.

He will tell you what he's made today. You will choose. You will wait. And then you will understand why people come back every time they're in Vienna. The tiramisu alone — made from the original Treviso recipe — has been known to silence entire tables.

— Vienna, Austria
